Our Story

Founded in 2005, Fat to Fit has been serving authentic cuisine made from locally-sourced ingredients. Our chef, Marco Bianchi, brings over 20 years of culinary experience from Italy and France to create dishes that delight the senses.

We believe in sustainable practices and supporting local farmers and producers. Every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and innovation.
